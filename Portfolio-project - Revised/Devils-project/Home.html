Martin Brodeur

Martin Brodeur was born on May 6th, 1972 in Montreal Canada. In the 1991-1992 NHL season he was drafted by the New Jersey Devils, playing 4 games that season including one playoff game. He would spend almost 25 years in the NHL, mostly with the New Jersey Devils. On Febuary 9th, 2016 his number #30 was retired by the Devils, marking the end of his playing career in the NHL.
